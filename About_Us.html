About Us


We are a family owned business born and raised in Southern California. Now operating within the Blue Ridge mountain's of Wise, Virginia. We were inspired by the microbreweries that we grew up with. These experiences are what we wanted to bring to the surrounding areas. We specialize in brewing dark beers and rotate seasonal brews for our customer's different tastes. We want to provide a community based business that operates on the premise of always providing the best Customer Service with the Best Beer.

Mission - To brew the best Dark Beer in the Blue Ridge mountains

Vision - To grow with the community as an integrated business that speaks for who we are as the business and as a representative of the community
